Mammals

Three identifing factors of mammals that are not found in other animals:

  • Three middle ear bones
  • Fur or hair
  • The females produce milk

Being warm-blooded mammals can adapt to many different environments.

Birds

The main identifying feature of birds is that they have feathers.

Even though all birds have feathers, not all birds can fly.

Reptiles

Two identifying features of reptiles are:

  • They are cold-blooded
  • They have scales

Because they are cold-blooded, their body temperature is controlled by their environment.
